# **A) WELCOME MESSAGE**

## **What is AiduxCare North?**

AiduxCare North is an AI-powered clinical documentation assistant designed specifically for Canadian physiotherapists. It helps you create accurate SOAP notes faster, allowing you to focus more time on patient care.

## **Pilot Objective**

We're testing AiduxCare North with a select group of Canadian physiotherapists to:
- Validate clinical accuracy
- Measure time savings
- Gather feedback for improvements
- Ensure PHIPA compliance

## **Estimated Time per Visit**

- **Recording:** 2-5 minutes
- **SOAP Generation:** 30 seconds (automated)
- **Review & Edit:** 2-3 minutes
- **Total Time Saved:** 5-10 minutes per visit

## **What We Expect from You**

- Use AiduxCare for at least 10 patient visits during the pilot
- Provide honest feedback via the in-app widget
- Report any issues or concerns immediately
- Complete the end-of-pilot survey

# **B) QUICK START INSTRUCTIONS (1 PAGE)**

## **Step 1: Create Patient**

1. Go to Command Center
2. Click "New Patient"
3. Enter patient information
4. Save patient record

## **Step 2: Start Recording**

1. Select patient from dropdown
2. Click "Start Recording"
3. Grant microphone permissions if prompted
4. Conduct your session normally
5. Click "Stop Recording" when finished

## **Step 3: Generate SOAP**

1. Review the transcript (if needed)
2. Click "Analyze" to generate clinical analysis
3. Click "Generate SOAP Note"
4. Wait ~30 seconds for processing

## **Step 4: Review**

1. Review the generated SOAP note
2. Edit any sections as needed
3. Verify clinical accuracy
4. Add any missing information

## **Step 5: Save to Vault**

1. Click "Finalize & Save"
2. Note is automatically saved to Clinical Vault
3. Access anytime from `/documents`

## **Step 6: Copy to EMR**

1. Go to Clinical Vault (`/documents`)
2. Find your note
3. Click "Copy to Clipboard"
4. Paste into your EMR system

# **D) FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS**

## **Where is my data stored?**

All data is stored in **Canadian data centers** (Firestore, Storage, Functions). We comply with PHIPA (Personal Health Information Protection Act) requirements.

## **How does AiduxCare protect my data?**

- All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- Access restricted to authorized personnel only
- Audit trail of all data access
- PHIPA-compliant data handling
- No data shared with third parties without consent

## **What happens if audio recording fails?**

- Automatic retry mechanism attempts to re-upload
- Clear error message displayed
- Option to re-record if needed
- Manual entry fallback available

## **Can I edit SOAP notes after generation?**

Yes! You can edit any section of the SOAP note before finalizing. Changes are saved automatically.

## **How do I access my saved notes?**

All notes are saved in the **Clinical Vault** (`/documents`). You can search by patient name, date, or keywords.

## **What if I need help?**

- Use the in-app feedback widget
- Email support@aiduxcare.com
- Check the FAQ section
- Review this welcome pack

# **E) KNOWN LIMITATIONS (TRANSPARENCY)**

## **Beta Version**

This is a **beta version** of AiduxCare North. Some features may not work perfectly, and we appreciate your patience as we improve.

## **Possible Issues**

- **Audio quality:** Poor audio quality may affect transcription accuracy
- **Processing delays:** Occasional delays during peak usage
- **Mobile compatibility:** Some features may work better on desktop
- **AI accuracy:** AI-generated content requires clinical review

## **What to Do If Something Fails**

1. **Don't panic** - Your data is safe
2. **Try again** - Many issues resolve with a retry
3. **Report it** - Use the feedback widget immediately
4. **Use fallback** - Manual entry is always available
5. **Contact support** - Email support@aiduxcare.com if urgent
