## 1. Introduction

This guide explains how to use AiDuxCare for clinical documentation and session management. AiDuxCare is designed to reduce documentation friction while maintaining professional standards and compliance. All clinical responsibility remains with the treating professional.

---

## 2. What AiDuxCare Is

AiDuxCare is a clinical documentation support tool. Supported visit types: Initial Assessment, Follow-up (requires baseline), Ongoing (first time in AiDuxCare). AiDuxCare does not diagnose, recommend treatments, or replace clinical judgment.

---

## 3. Access and Login

Navigate to https://pilot.aiduxcare.com, complete Cloudflare Access (email + one-time code), sign in with AiDuxCare credentials. Requirements: Chrome/Firefox/Safari/Edge; internet; microphone.

## 5. Session Types

Initial Assessment — Close to establish baseline. Follow-up — Requires baseline. Ongoing — Complete Ongoing intake form first.

## 6. Today's Patients

Status: Pending (blue), Done (grey), Overdue (red). Start, trash, calendar for planning.

---

## 7. Work with Patients

New: Initial Assessment or Ongoing. Existing: Initial, Follow-up, or Ongoing intake. Add to today.

---

## 8. Ongoing Intake Form

Sections: Patient record (new only), Chief complaint & subjective, **Previous history** (with **Add documents** for images/PDFs), Objective, Clinical impression, Plan. Dictation via device microphone (Web Speech API). Minimum: Chief complaint + (impression OR plan).
